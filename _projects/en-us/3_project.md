<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Project Goal:</h3>
<ul>
	<li>To develop, create, and assemble a prototype of an anthropomorphic intelligent robot capable of performing
		movements (walking on two legs, arm movement, head movement), capable of analyzing and processing external
		information, demonstrating intelligent behavior similar to human behavior in similar situations, making decisions,
		and reacting to changes in the surrounding environment.</li>
</ul>
<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Project Objectives:</h3>
<ul>
	<li>To develop, design, create, and assemble a prototype of an anthropomorphic intelligent robot.</li>
	<li>To develop an intelligent control platform.</li>
	<li>Additionally: to create an educational project for training, testing, and various research.</li>
</ul>
<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Project Hypothesis:</h3>
<p>Robotics is an irreversible process that awaits us in the future; we will live with robots.</p>
<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Relevance:</h3>
<p>A person always needs an assistant.</p>
