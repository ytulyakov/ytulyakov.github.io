<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Project Goal:</h3>
<ul>
    <li>To develop, create, and assemble a prototype of an intelligent humanoid robot capable of performing movements (walking on two legs, arm movements, head movements), analyzing and processing external information, demonstrating intelligent behavior similar to human behavior in similar situations, making decisions, and responding to changes in the environment.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Project Objectives:</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Develop, design, create, and assemble a prototype of the intelligent humanoid robot.</li>
    <li>Develop an intelligent control platform.</li>
    <li>Additionally: create an educational project for training, testing, and various research.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Project Hypothesis:</h3>
<p>Robotics is an irreversible process that awaits us in the future; we will live with robots.</p>

<h3 style="font-weight: normal;">Relevance:</h3>
<p>Humans always need a helper.</p>
