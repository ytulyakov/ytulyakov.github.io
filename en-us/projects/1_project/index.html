Industrial robot

Intelligent industrial robot (using artificial intelligence, machine learning, neural networks, machine vision technologies - Industry 4.0)

Project Name:

Intelligent industrial robot (using artificial intelligence, machine learning, neural networks, machine vision technologies - Industry 4.0)

Application:

  • Packing, loading and unloading, palletizing.
  • Selecting, moving, locating facility (machine tool maintenance).
  • Manufacturing and Assembly.
  • Welding.
Prototype of an intelligent industrial robot

Project Objective:

  • Design, create, assemble an industrial robot that would have a wide range of functional capabilities and could perform both basic and auxiliary tasks (universal) with intelligent control that is capable of analyzing and processing external information and demonstrating intelligent behavior similar to human behavior in similar situations, making complex decisions and responding to changes in the environment.
  • To draw attention, justify, prove the necessity of industrial robots production in Belarus and their introduction into production processes.

Project Objective:

  • Developing, designing, assembling a prototype of an intelligent industrial robot, conducting research, testing, drawing conclusions.
  • Development of an intelligent platform - an industrial robot control platform that will be applicable and capable in controlling any type of industrial robots and any number of industrial robots using artificial intelligence, machine learning, neural network, and machine vision technologies.
  • Developing, designing, assembling a prototype of an intelligent industrial robot, conducting research, testing, and formulating conclusions.
  • Developing, designing, assembling a lineup of intelligent industrial robots, conducting research, testing, and drawing conclusions.
  • Organization of assembly of intelligent industrial robots, introduction to the market of the Republic of Belarus.
  • Organization of serial production of intelligent industrial robots, launch on domestic and foreign markets.
  • Optional: development of an educational project.

Project Hypothesis:

Intelligent industrial robots can be used wherever there is work for humans to do.

Relevance:

Industrial robotics will ensure the competitiveness of the national economy of Belarus in the global arena, and the production of industrial robots will strengthen the technological sovereignty of Belarus.
